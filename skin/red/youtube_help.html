Directions:

  • Upload your video to YouTube. If you don’t have an account set up, you will need to do so (www.youtube.com)
  • Once your video is uploaded, click on your user name in the upper right corner of the screen and select “My Videos.”
  • Click on the title of the video that you’d like to upload to your SOC Site.
  • Copy the code from the “Embed” field on the right side of the screen.
  • Go to “Your Admin” page on www.socexchange.com.au and click “Step 4: Add/Edit – all items”.
  • Enter in all the necessary information required to complete your listing.
    The SOC  View video facility can be used for each listing in Step 4, or it can also be displayed on your
    Food Marketplace Homepage by inserting the code in Step 2.
  • Near the bottom of the page, you can paste the code from YouTube in the “YouTube Video” Box.
  • Click “Save to my Website.”
